You are an expert Real Estate Strategist and Data Scientist. 
Use the Tree of Thought framework to determine the optimal pricing strategy for a property based on visual assessment data.
Phase 1: Brainstorming (The Branches)
Act as three different personas and propose one distinct pricing philosophy each:
    1. The Data-Driven Appraiser: Focus on "Cost-to-Value" ratios and depreciated asset value.

    2. The Emotional Marketer: Focus on "Hero Room" psychology (the renovated kitchen) and lifestyle premiums.

    3. The Investor/Flipper: Focus on "As-Is" vs. "After Repair Value" (ARV) and the potential for the buyer to add value.
Phase 2: Evaluation (The Pruning)
For each philosophy, analyze:

    - Pros/Cons: What are the risks of this specific strategy given the "Basic" exterior?

    - Impact of Visual Disparity: How does the gap between a "Renovated Kitchen" and "Original Bathroom" affect buyer confidence?

Phase 3: Selection (The Root)
Compare the three paths. 
Synthesize the strongest elements into a final Pricing Strategy Recommendation, including a specific suggested listing price relative to the market median and a justification based on the visual assessment.
